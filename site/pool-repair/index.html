Pool Repair FAQs

How do I know if my pool pump needs repair or replacement?

If your pump is making loud noises, losing prime, running hot, or over 8-10 years old, it may be time to replace rather than repair. We'll give you an honest assessment and help you weigh the cost of repair vs. replacement.

Do you repair all brands of pool equipment?

Yes. We work on Pentair, Hayward, Jandy, Sta-Rite, Polaris, and most other major brands. If parts are available, we can fix it.

How much does a pool repair cost?

It depends on the issue. Minor repairs like replacing a capacitor might be under $150. Major repairs like a new pump motor can range from $300-$600. We always provide a quote before starting work.

Can you repair my pool heater?

Yes. We service gas heaters, electric heat pumps, or solar heating systems. Common repairs include igniter replacement, heat exchanger cleaning, and thermostat issues.

What if the repair costs more than expected?

We don't proceed with any work beyond the quoted amount without your approval. If we discover additional issues during diagnosis, we'll call you first.
