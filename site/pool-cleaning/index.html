Pool Cleaning FAQs

How often should I have my pool cleaned in Florida?

For most Vero Beach pools, weekly cleaning is ideal. Florida's heat, humidity, and frequent rain create perfect conditions for algae growth. Pools with screen enclosures and light use may be fine with bi-weekly service.

Do you bring your own chemicals?

Yes, we bring everything needed to balance your water. Chemicals are included in your service cost—no surprise fees.

What if it rains on my service day?

Light rain doesn't stop us. If there's severe weather, we'll reschedule for the next available day and let you know.

Do you service saltwater pools?

Absolutely. Our technicians are experienced with both saltwater and traditional chlorine pools. We'll check your salt cell and chlorinator as part of regular service.

Can you clean a green pool?

Yes. If your pool has turned green, we offer one-time green pool cleanup to restore it. Once it's clear, we can set you up on regular service to keep it that way.
