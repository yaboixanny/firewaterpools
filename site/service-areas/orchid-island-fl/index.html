Pool Service FAQs for Orchid Island Residents

Are you familiar with Orchid Island community procedures?

Yes. We service multiple pools within Orchid Island and are familiar with gate access, community guidelines, and the expectations of residents here.

How do you handle the salt air effects on pool equipment?

Salt air accelerates corrosion on metal components. We inspect equipment for early signs of corrosion, recommend protective measures like sacrificial anodes, and advise on equipment choices that hold up better in coastal environments.

Can you service our pool while we're at our other residence?

Absolutely. Many Orchid Island clients are seasonal. We provide full-service maintenance year-round and can coordinate with your property manager or send you photos and reports after each visit.
