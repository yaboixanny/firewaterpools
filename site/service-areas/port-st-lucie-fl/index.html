Pool Services in Port St. Lucie

Complete pool care solutions for all Port St. Lucie neighborhoods.

Pool Cleaning

We provide reliable weekly service to keep your water crystal clear and swim-ready all year long. Our team handles everything from skimming and vacuuming to brushing the walls and precise chemical balancing. You simply enjoy your pool without the hassle of doing the chores yourself.

Pool Maintenance

Regular maintenance is essential for extending the lifespan of your entire pool system. We perform routine filter cleanings and detailed equipment inspections to catch small mechanical issues before they fail. This proactive approach helps you avoid expensive emergency repairs down the road.

Pool Repair

When equipment breaks down, our licensed technicians provide fast and accurate diagnostics to get you back up and running. We specialize in repairing all major brands of pumps, motors, filters, and heaters with minimal downtime. Let us restore your pool’s function quickly so you don’t miss a day of swimming.

Equipment Installation

Upgrade your current setup with modern, energy-efficient equipment that significantly lowers your monthly utility bills. We install high-performance variable speed pumps, salt chlorine generators, and smart automation systems. Control your entire pool directly from your smartphone with our expert installation services.

Green Pool Cleanup

Don’t let a swampy pool become a permanent breeding ground for mosquitoes and bacteria. Our aggressive green-to-clean treatments shock the water and remove algae blooms rapidly to make the water safe again. We work hard to restore your pool's health, clarity, and balance in record time.

Leak Detection

Unexplained water loss can damage your property foundation and drastically spike your water bill. We utilize advanced electronic listening devices and pressure testing to pinpoint the exact location of the leak. Our non-invasive methods ensure we find the problem without tearing up your deck unnecessarily.

PSL Pool Service FAQs

Do you service master-planned communities like Tradition?

Yes, we service all gated and non-gated communities in Tradition, St. Lucie West, and throughout Port St. Lucie.

What is included in weekly service?

Our weekly service includes skimming, brushing walls, vacuuming (as needed), emptying baskets, cleaning the filter, and balancing all chemicals.

How quickly can you fix a broken pump?

We prioritize repairs for our regular service customers. Often we can diagnose and repair or replace equipment within 24-48 hours.

How much does weekly pool service cost in Port St. Lucie?

Pricing depends on pool size, system type, and condition. Most PSL homeowners invest between $175 - $350+ monthly for weekly service.

Yes — most PSL pools are screened, which helps reduce debris but still requires weekly chemical balancing and cleaning.
